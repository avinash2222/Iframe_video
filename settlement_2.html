DroneMaps for the settlements of the future

Smart Cities/villages Applications supports 2D and 3D maps, automated geotagging of assets, and integration with IoTs.





Pipeline Planning



CAD compatible extractions for planning optimisation. Application enables tracking on the length of road,type, and junction elevations.

Property Tax Estimations



Platform support for planning of closed range flights to procure facade data. Geotagging of assets and number of floors in properties.

Change Tracking



Support for timelines and multiple mappings, with automated change detection in geotagged assets.

Flood Mapping



Applications for predictive model for floods that relies on dense elevation data. Drone based 3D models also take into account the temporary urban settlements and lead to better planning.
